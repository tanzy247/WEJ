Mission Beach

Mission Beach

Here we took a lovely stroll on the golden sand, enjoying the sunshine. Cutting inland, we were lucky enough to encounter a cassowary - a rare, endangered flightless bird.

Waterfall Circuit

Waterfall Circuit

From Mission beach we travelled up to Port Douglas. Completing the waterfall circuit en route.

Port Douglas

Port Douglas

Whilst in Port Douglas we had some chilled time, and used it as a base to explore the Daintree Rainforest - The Oldest Rainforest on earth.

Daintree Rainforest - Day 1

Day 1

We visited the Daintree Discovery centre which allowed us to explore the natural rainforest but with a headset talking us through the different plants, trees and animals.

As well as this we strolled along the beach known to be ‘where the rainforest meets the reef’.

Daintree Rainforest - Day 2

Day 2

We visited a different area of the rainforest and went for a walk at our own leisure.

Cairns

Cairns

One of the main reasons we wanted to visit Australia - The Great Barrier Reef - and it didn’t disappoint.

It was amazing to see all of the coral, as well as hundreds of different types of fish. We were lucky enough to also spot a stingray, a lobster and a Whitetip reef shark.

We spent 2 days diving, completing 5 dives as part of our advanced divers course and we are now qualified for deeper dives up to 30m.
