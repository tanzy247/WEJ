Mexico

Chichen Itza

Chichen Itza

This was actually not as impressive as the previous ruins we had seen, which made me curious as to why it is one of the 7 wonders of the world. We found out that this is due to the use of the temple. The temple was used as a calendar.

The temple is split it to 4 sections made up of 90 steps. These steps each represent a day and each row represents a season. They would use this to work out the season and know where was best to harvest certain foods etc. There is then a small section at the top to represent the 5 remaining days.

Day 1

Day 2

Day 2

We hired a car and travelled outside of Vegas.

First we visited Boulder City, which is where the workers and families lived who built Hoover Dam. We visited a museum which had an amazing informative video. We were so impressed with the engineering - particularly at the time with the resources in the middle of the dessert - but also because of the impact the dam had on the whole of America, preventing floods and improving farming even down to Mexico.
