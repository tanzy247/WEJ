Mexico City

Mexico City

Next we headed to Mexico City. On the first day we took part in a city walking tour to learn the history from the Aztecs to now.

Mexico City

That evening we immersed ourselves into Mexican culture. Listening to their music, trying their drinks and watching their local entertainment.

We went to the oldest arena in the city to watch Lucha Libre - freestyle wrestling.

Teotihuacan

Teotihuacan

Whilst in Mexico we took part in a trip to visit some nearby ruins. The temple of the sun and the temple of the moon.

San Cristobal

San Cristobal

After Mexico City, we headed to a small town called San Cristobal. Whilst we were there they were celebrating a religious festival.

Palenque

Palenque

We did a day trip from San Cristobal to visit Palenque ruins and Agua Azul - a waterfall.#

The Palenque ruins were really impressive to see. They are the remains from a Mayan city; however, it was abandoned and overgrown with jungle. 90% of the city still remains under cover from the jungle.
