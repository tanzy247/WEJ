Malaysia

Penang

Penang

Our first stop in Malaysia was Penang where we explored George Town - famous for its Colonial buildings and street art (being the first British settlement in Southeast Asia).

Cameron Highlands

Cameron Highlands

After George Town, we headed to the Cameron Highlands for a day. Here we enjoyed visiting a tea plantation and strawberry farm. We also visited local markets and enjoyed sampling the different fresh strawberry products.

Kuala Lumpur

Kuala Lumpur

Next we headed to the capital, Kuala Lumpur. We made an obligatory visit to the famous Petronas Twin Towers - the tallest in the world and watched as the sun set and the lights came on!

Batu Caves

Batu Caves

On our second day in Kuala Lumpur, we visited the famous Batu caves and enjoyed sampling local street food.

Tioman Island

Tioman Island

Our final stop in Malaysia was Tioman Island. Again, we spent most of our time here under water; however, we thoroughly enjoyed diving and enjoying some relaxing time on the beach. Whilst diving we saw hawksbill turtles, reef sharks, spotted stingray, angel fish, butterfly fish, scorpion fish, clown fish, bat fish, trigger fish, puffer fish, barracuda and many others.
