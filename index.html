Komodo Islands

Day 1

Day 1

We left Bali behind and headed for the Komodo Islands. Flying to Labuan Bajo (The nearest area to get a boat to visit the islands). We explored the local town. In the evening we were pleasantly surprised to watch two local teams playing a 5-a-side football match. We couldn’t believe they had an audience as well as official refs and a commentator; however, we really enjoyed watching the local people living their normal lives. For dinner we tried different street food from the local markets.

Day 2

Day 2

We were super excited to go diving in the Komodo islands, because we had heard such great things. We were not disappointed!

Our first day was at a sight called Police Corner, which had lots of amazing coral - we also got to see a few reef white tipped sharks.

Our second dive was at a manta ray cleaning station, which was absolutely incredible to see. I couldn’t believe how many there were and how huge they are. It was so incredible to see them so close!

Our third and final dive of the day was another successful dive where we saw different fish, an eel and lobster.

Day 3

Day 3

We had an early start today and headed out on another boat to visit the land of Komodo Island and spot some Komodo dragons. We were really lucky to see a baby, a female and a large male. I couldn’t believe the size of the male.

After visiting the dragons we spent the afternoon snorkelling at different spots and again were able to see the manta rays as well as turtles.

Singapore

Day 1

Day 1

We arrived in Singapore in the evening and were pleased to be reunited with Julie and Yorkie at the airport. That evening we headed out to a food market to explore the hustle and bustle.

Day 2 - Morning

Day 2

In the morning, we explored the gardens by the bay and headed up to the top of the iconic Marina Bay Sands to enjoy a drink with views of the gardens.

Day 2 - Afternoon

Day 2

In the afternoon, we continued to explore the city highlights including the floral and botanical gardens.

Day 2 - Evening

Day 2

In the evening, we ventured to China Town and Little Indian.

Day 3

Day 3

First we enjoyed a Singapore sling in the Raffles Hotel, before heading over to the Sentosa Island via Cable Car. When we arrived we enjoyed racing each other on the luges, and then visited the Fort where British soldiers were posted during the war.
