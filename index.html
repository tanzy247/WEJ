Rainbow Mountain

Rainbow Mountain

Hiking to Rainbow Mountain

It is 5036m above sea level. The views were lovely but the altitude was tricky.

Colca Canyon

Colca Canyon

Watching Andean condors fly through the canyon.

Lake Titicaca

Lake Titicaca

We travelled by boat to some floating islands.
This was absolutely amazing. They make these islands themselves from soil and reeds. 4 families then live on each island. We also got to go on the reed boats, which they also make by hand and last 1 year.

Amantani Island

Amantani Island

Visiting an actual island, where we stayed with a local family in their home. We hiked up to 2 temples at the top of the island.

Party Time!

In the evening we took part in a traditional party wearing their hand made local outfits.

Taquile Island

Taquile Island

Visiting a different island the next day.
We got to see more traditional dances and also see how they use natural plants to make shampoos and clean sheep’s wool to then make clothes.
