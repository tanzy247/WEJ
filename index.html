Iguazu Falls - Argentina Side

Iguazu Falls - Argentina Side

By far one of the best days yet. The falls are absolutely incredible to see, feel and listen to.

We went on the boat trip, which was one of the best decisions ever. Seeing the falls close up, and going inside them and getting absolutely soaked!

The water speed was 10 milliom cubic metres per second and it is normally 1-2.
