Bali

Ubud - Day 1

Day 1

On our first day in Ubud we explored the city and visited monkey forest. We enjoyed walking around the markets, and in the evening treated ourselves to a full body massage for just 3 pounds - bargain!

Ubud - Day 2

Day 2

We hired a scooter each and explored the rice fields, rice terraces and local temples.

We also tried some new fruits. We really enjoyed snake fruit and mangosteens but we were not keen on the durian.

Ubud - Day 3

Day 3

We hired a scooter again to explore the less touristy northern side. We visited Mount Batur (a dormant volcano) and enjoyed the local hot springs.

In the evening, we attended a jewellery making class, which was super fun. We worked alongside the professionals to make a ring from scratch, which we engraved with our initials and now have as a keepsake.

Lombok

Lombok

We then headed to Lombok - a nearby island - where we hired scooters again to explore. We visited a local village - Sade. This is where the native people of Lombok live. They live off the land: growing their own rice and vegetables and making their own homes, tools etc.

There were plenty of peaceful beaches.

Gili Air Island

Gili Air Island

From Lombok we boarded a small public boat filled with supplies for the island. Gili Air island is a tiny island which has no roads and can be easily walked/biked in a day. Their main mode of transport is horse and cart.
