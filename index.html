Santiago

Santiago

We spent a few days here, beginning with a city tour to learn more about the history of Santiago. A funicular journey took us up to the San Cristobal Statue where we had great views over the city. We also went on a cable car to get views of the other side. We spent some time in one of their parks in the city, which is on the top off a hill so you can walk up and see over the city. Finally, a hike in a nearby area called San Hose.

Valparaiso

Valparaiso

We spent a day and night on the coast, west of Santiago, in a place called Valparaiso - famous for street art. It also had the best carrot cake and Oreo cheesecake EVER!

Vina del Mar

Vina del Mar

Just along the coast from Valparaiso, we visited Vina del Mar for a day. It has some lovely beaches, a cute little castle and some sand dunes where we watched the sunset.
