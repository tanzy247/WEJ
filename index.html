San Pedro de Atacama - Chile

San Pedro de Atacama

San Pedro de Atacama is a small tourist town in the middle of the Atacama desert.

Stargazing

The first night we went star gazing.
An expert astrologist pointed out different signs including Scorpio, Pisces, Sagittarius, Aries and Gemini.
He also taught us how to spot how old a star is and we got to look through telescopes! We saw Saturn and Jupiter - Saturn being my favourite.
You could see the ring around it!

Lagoons

Day 2

The second day we did a trip to some lagoons and we got to swim in one that is so salty you just float, like the Dead Sea.

Hot Springs and Geysers

Day 3

An early and cold start. We went to visit the hot springs and geysers - absolutely incredible. They are formed naturally and boil water from volcanic activity. A very chilly -10!

Relaxing

Time to Relax!

After a busy month, time to slow down and take it easy for a few days.
