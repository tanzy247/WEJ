Ilha Grande

We spent 2 days winding down on an island off Rio called Ilha Grande. We visited some incredible beaches and lagoons with gorgeous clear waters. One of my favourite things being seeing turtle swimming whilst we were snorkelling. We also visited a cave.

The Acaiá Cave hides a wonderful spectacle inside. Approximately 8 meters below sea level, there is a crack in the coastal stone, through which seawater has access to an underground hall where there is an underground stone beach. Sunlight propagated through water forms the phenomenon of fluorescence. We were told there are only three caves like it in the world.
