Chiang Mai

Our first stop in Thailand was Chiang Mai. We were really exited to visit Thailand, as Thai food is our favourite cuisine, so we decided to start with a cooking class. As always the food was absolutely delicious and luckily we have the recipes so we can try to replicate the flavours at home. We made Pad Thai, red and green curries, papaya salad, coconut milk soup and mango sticky rice.

Bangkok

After exploring the north, we headed down to Bangkok where we took part in a day trip to visit the famous death railway. First we visited the cemetery, followed by the museum where we learnt more about the prisoners of war, who were forced to build the railway. We also learnt about the bombings on the bridge over the River Kwai. We then took a scenic ride on the train across death railway.

Koh Tao

Koh Tao

Sadly this is the only photo I took in Koh Tao, but this is only because we spent most of our time here underwater. We spent 2 days diving, as well as a day snorkelling. We got to see lots of fantastic coral, fish as well as two giant green turtles.
